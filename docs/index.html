REIR Calculator

A clinical education tool for re-irradiation EQD2 calculations and allowable dose estimation.

Educational Use Only

About

REIR Calculator is an iOS application designed for educational purposes in radiation oncology. It assists clinicians and students in understanding the radiobiological principles behind re-irradiation planning by computing EQD2 (Equivalent Dose in 2 Gy fractions) values for previously irradiated organs at risk (OARs).

Given the doses delivered in prior treatment courses and the desired number of fractions for a new course, the app calculates the remaining allowable EQD2 for selected OARs and derives the corresponding total dose and dose per fraction that could be safely considered for re-irradiation — based on established radiobiological tolerance data.

Key Features

EQD2 Calculation

Calculates cumulative EQD2 for two prior treatment courses using the linear-quadratic model.

OAR Tolerance Limits

Compares cumulative dose against AAPM/ASTRO re-irradiation tolerance limits for major OARs.

Allowable Dose Estimation

Estimates total dose and dose per fraction for a new course based on remaining EQD2 budget and desired fraction count.

Time-Based Reduction

Applies time-interval dose recovery corrections between treatment courses.

Manual α/β Selection

Supports custom α/β ratio input for flexible radiobiological modeling.

Light & Dark Mode

Fully adapts to iOS system appearance for comfortable use in any environment.

Important Notice: REIR Calculator is intended solely for educational and research purposes. It is not a substitute for clinical judgment, institutional protocols, or physician decision-making. All clinical decisions must be made by qualified healthcare professionals.
